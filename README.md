📄 Pages you should create (important)

You don’t need anything fancy — just basic, clear pages:

1. Privacy Policy (/privacy)

Include:

what data you collect (even if minimal)
analytics usage
cookies (or “no cookies” if true)
how users can contact you
2. Terms of Service (/terms)

Include:

users use at their own risk
you can change or stop the service
no guarantees (uptime, accuracy, etc.)
limitation of liability
3. Cookies Policy (/cookies)

Even if you say “no cookies”:

state it clearly
mention third-party services if any


You get referrer once per page load,
but for your product you should capture it once per user session and store it
